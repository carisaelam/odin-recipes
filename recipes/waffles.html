Waffles

Description

Crisp on the outside, fluffy on the inside, waffles are a delightful breakfast treat loved by all. As the batter hits the hot waffle iron, it begins to bubble and cook, filling the kitchen with a tantalizing aroma. Each bite offers a perfect balance of sweetness and texture, making waffles a beloved morning tradition for many.

Whether topped with a drizzle of maple syrup, a dollop of whipped cream, or fresh fruit, waffles are endlessly customizable to suit any taste. From classic buttermilk waffles to indulgent chocolate chip or savory bacon and cheese varieties, there's a waffle recipe to satisfy every craving.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter (melted)
  • Vanilla extract
  • Optional toppings: maple syrup, whipped cream, fresh fruit, chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat your waffle iron according to manufacturer instructions.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs and then add the milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Mix until well combined.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; some lumps are okay.
  5. Spray the waffle iron with non-stick cooking spray or brush with melted butter.
  6. Pour enough batter onto the hot waffle iron to cover the grids, then close the lid and cook according to manufacturer instructions, until the waffles are golden brown and crispy.
  7. Carefully remove the waffles from the iron and serve immediately with your favorite toppings.
  8. Enjoy your delicious homemade waffles!
